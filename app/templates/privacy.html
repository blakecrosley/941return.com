Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Return... is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your meditation practice is personal, and your data should stay that way.

What We Don't Collect

Return... does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. We have no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Data Stored on Your Device

Return... stores your preferences and settings locally on your device. This includes:

  • Timer duration preferences
  • Selected theme (Water, Fire, Forest, Silence)
  • Bell sound and repeat settings
  • HealthKit logging preference

This data never leaves your device except through iCloud sync (described below).

Apple Health Integration

If you enable HealthKit integration, Return... can log your meditation sessions as "Mindful Minutes" to Apple Health. This data:

  • Is stored in Apple Health on your device
  • Is managed by Apple's Health app
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time through Apple Health

You can disable HealthKit integration at any time in the app's settings.

iCloud Sync

Return... uses iCloud Key-Value Store to sync your settings and session history across your Apple devices. This means:

  • Your data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Only you have access to this data
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • You can manage or delete this data through your iCloud settings

No Third-Party Services

Return... is a standalone app with no external dependencies for data processing. We use:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations

Children's Privacy

Return... does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at blake@941apps.com.

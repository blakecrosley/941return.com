Getting Started

Return offers a free tier with basic timer functionality. A one-time purchase unlocks all themes (Water, Fire, Forest, Silence), premium bell sounds, and advanced features like repeat modes. No subscriptions, ever.

Return works across the entire Apple ecosystem:

  • iPhone — Full experience with Live Activities and Dynamic Island
  • Apple Watch — Start sessions from your wrist with haptic notifications
  • Mac — Full-screen immersive mode for desktop meditation
  • Apple TV — Living room meditation with 4K ambient backgrounds

Privacy & Data

Nothing. Return collects zero personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs. Your meditation practice is private—we don't know who you are or how often you meditate.

Your settings and session history sync automatically via iCloud. This means your preferred theme, duration, and streak carry across all your Apple devices. The sync happens directly through Apple's infrastructure—we never see your data.
